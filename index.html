About the Conference

The International Conference on Frontiers in Electronics and Smart Technologies (ICFEST-2025) will be held on May 30-31, 2025, at Srinivas Institute of Technology, Mangalore, Karnataka, India. This event is dedicated to advancing innovation and promoting research in various domains of Electronics and Smart Technologies. With the rapid evolution of these fields, the conference aims to provide a platform for researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and students to exchange ideas and present their research findings.

ICFEST-2025 focuses on six core thematic tracks: VLSI, IoT, Embedded Systems, Machine Learning, Cybersecurity, and Power Systems. These tracks represent key areas driving advancements in technology and their application to solving modern challenges.

The conference will feature a series of keynote presentations, technical papers, and poster sessions designed to foster dialogue and collaboration among participants. Researchers from diverse specializations will discuss emerging trends, explore solutions to complex problems, and share insights into the development of efficient and economical systems. This aligns with the objective of providing a platform for technical innovation and interdisciplinary research.

ICFEST-2025 recognizes the importance of collaborative expertise in achieving breakthroughs in engineering and technology. Advances in fields such as VLSI, IoT, Embedded Systems, and Machine Learning have paved the way for smarter, more connected, and energy-efficient systems. Similarly, developments in Cybersecurity and Power Systems are critical to addressing security and energy challenges in today’s world.

ICFEST-2025 is committed to nurturing research scholars, postgraduate students, and industry professionals by providing a platform for presenting their findings and engaging in meaningful discussions. By fostering collaboration and knowledge-sharing, the conference seeks to drive innovation and contribute to technological development that benefits society as a whole.

Select papers from the ICFEST-2025 conference will be published in Scopus-indexed proceedings. Authors must ensure the quality of their submissions.
